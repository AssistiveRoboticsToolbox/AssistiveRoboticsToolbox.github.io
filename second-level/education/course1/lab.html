Mission

Develop enabling technologies for assistive and surgical robotics, and apply that knowledge to advance the science of biomechanics and biomedical engineering · Learn and practice sensors, actuators, and robots · Learn soft robots · Practice skills: electronics, circuit design, wireless communication, programming (C, Python), mechanical design · Work with medical doctors and industry · Hands-on lab sessions to practice · Work in teams and communicate results
